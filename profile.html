About Me

Ph.D. Candidate, Aerospace Engineering – University of Cincinnati

I investigate high‑speed flows, non‑equilibrium gas dynamics, and propulsion, with an emphasis on CFD methods that accurately capture the coupled physics of shock‑wave/boundary‑layer interactions, cavity acoustics, and reactive chemistry.

My background spans compressible flow modeling, interface‑resolved multiphase simulation, and scramjet inlet design. I am passionate about translating fundamental insights into practical hypersonic vehicle technologies.

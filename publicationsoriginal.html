Vortex behavior in a dynamically moving hypersonic cavity

This study uses direct numerical simulation to uncover the dominant flow physics when a Mach‑11 stream interacts with a cavity whose length‑to‑depth ratio actively changes from 2 to 9.8. Results reveal persistent shear‑layer detachment, vortex breakdown reminiscent of turbulence energy cascades, and sustained acoustic reflections.
